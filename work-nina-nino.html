Logo Design · Visual Branding

Nina Nino

Artisan logo design.

Year 2024
Industry Material Design Artist
Team Bella Wood

Overview

Nina Nino is a talented interior design artisan who creates bespoke, handcrafted pieces and curated spaces. She needed a visual identity that would reflect her artistic vision and attention to detail while appealing to discerning clients who value unique, artisanal design work. The logo and branding needed to stand out in the competitive interior design market and communicate Nina's unique approach to creating one-of-a-kind spaces and pieces.

Outcome

  • Logo Design — Created an elegant, handcrafted-inspired wordmark that reflects the artisanal nature of Nina's work.
  • Visual Identity — Developed a cohesive branding system including color palette, typography, and visual elements.
  • Brand Guidelines — Established consistent application across all touchpoints from business cards to digital presence.
  • Material Focus — Emphasized natural textures and earthy tones that reflect the artisanal interior design aesthetic.
